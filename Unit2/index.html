Which two websites am I most inspired by?

I love the simplicity of Cuker Agency and Social Driver .

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Both utilizes a lot of clean white space, and have a grided structure with three or four columns.

What makes them similar and different?

Both have a similar style and I find myself liking simple, clean websites, that use a responsive design with grided content in utilizing parallax scrolling.

Cuker Interactive does a better job integrating their media. Rather than using embedded players and widgets, Cuker has a cleaner UI with their media player.
