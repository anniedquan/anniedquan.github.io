An American Original

Bourbon is a high-end cocktail concept with a speakeasy-style flair and a special ice-maker imported from Japan. Everything is made in-house daily -- from the syrups to the botanical essences -- and features locally sourced herbs. Most of the carefully crafted cocktails hail from from the Prohibition Era, thus are heavy on the whiskey (and light on the mixers). 150-strong whiskey and bourbon collection that features obscure selections and mainstreamers alike.

Take heed and order Bourbons's spin on an Old Fashioned, featuring Belle Meade bourbon, Laird's Applejack, smoked maple, and Angostura bitters.

Salt Lake City - Utah
